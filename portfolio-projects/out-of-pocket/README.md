# Out of Pocket: Phone Gone Wild

A fast-paced gesture game featuring 4 play modes, 14 gestures, and multiplayer experiences. Built end-to-end with SwiftUI and launched on the App Store.

## Project Details

- **Project Type:** Live iOS App
- **Tech Stack:** SwiftUI, iOS 17+, Analytics
- **Role:** Development Partner at Our Appologies LLC
- **Devices:** iPhone, iPad, Mac, Vision

## Game Features

- **4 Play Modes:** Classic Reflex, Memory, Pass & Play, and PVP
- **14 Gestures:** Swipes, taps, motion gestures, and Stroop-effect color challenges
- **Analytics:** Instrumented gameplay tracking for tuning and balancing
