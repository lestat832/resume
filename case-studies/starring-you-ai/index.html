Overview

Starring You AI represents a groundbreaking evolution in personalized video creation. As Product Manager, I led the development of this AI-powered feature that enables users to create customized video packs with their faces seamlessly integrated into JibJab's entertaining content.

The Challenge

JibJab needed to innovate beyond traditional face-in-hole technology to remain competitive in the evolving digital entertainment landscape. Users wanted more personalized, shareable content but found existing creation tools cumbersome and time-consuming.

Key Pain Points:

  • Complex user flows deterred content creation
  • Limited personalization options
  • Time-intensive process for creating multiple videos
  • Need to leverage emerging AI technology effectively

The Solution

I spearheaded the development of Starring You AI, an intuitive AI-powered product that revolutionized how users create personalized video content.

Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Face Integration: Advanced algorithms for seamless face placement
  • Pack Creation: Users can create multiple videos in one session
  • Simplified UX: Streamlined flow reducing creation time by 70%
  • Smart Recommendations: AI-driven content suggestions based on user preferences

My Approach:

  • Conducted extensive user research to identify friction points
  • Partnered with AI/ML team to define technical requirements
  • Led cross-functional team through agile development sprints
  • Implemented iterative testing with focus groups
  • Coordinated phased rollout strategy

Results & Impact

User Adoption

Generated over 400,000 user-created packs within the first 6 months, exceeding projections by 150%

Engagement Metrics

  • Increased user session time by 45%
  • Improved content creation rate by 3x
  • Achieved 82% user satisfaction score

Business Impact

  • Drove significant increase in premium subscriptions
  • Reduced customer support tickets by 30%
  • Established JibJab as an AI innovation leader

Key Learnings

  • User-Centric AI: Success comes from making AI invisible to users while delivering magical experiences
  • Iterative Development: Rapid prototyping and user testing were crucial for finding the right balance
  • Cross-functional Collaboration: Close partnership between product, engineering, and design was essential
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Continuous monitoring and optimization based on user behavior data
